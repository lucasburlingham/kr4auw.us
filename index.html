Welcome to one of my websites. My personal website can be found at lucasburlingham.me, and hosts a wider range of articles, strings around my online thumb, and an assortment of other Good Things.

You may have noticed that this website isn't amazing, besides the globe that greeted you. (Yes, those are accurate depictions of the QSO's I've made.)


That's on purpose.

What good is a hobby (such as amateur radio) if you get frustrated with it and spend so much time distracted that you never really enjoy the doing of the actual hobby?

That being said, it's a shame for a website to look like it was designed by a colorblind zebra who learned html4 and never turned back. Baaaackkk in myyyy dayyyy we used these things called TYPE WRITERS!

So here this site is. It's clean, simple, works decently well on a cell phone, and runs on a CDN so I don't have to worry about security.

How to Contact Me over the air.

I mostly run FT8. I'm a technician, so I'm limited to 10m on HF.

What about QSL Cards?

Never received one, but as soon as I do, I'll design one and get it in the mail using your return address.

What's the deal with the globe?

It's cool, right? It's a globe that shows the contacts I've made so far. You can make yours at /globegenerator by uploading an ADIF file of your own.
