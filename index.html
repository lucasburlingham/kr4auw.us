What equipment do I use?

I have a Xiegu G90, and use a 7 and 9Ah lead acid batteries for power. To charge it, I have a NOCO Genius 1.

I also have Baofeng DM-1701 and QRZ Jumpstart handhelds for 2m/70cm, and a GM-15 Pro that sits in my aid/go bag.

I wouldn't recommend a lead acid battery. Use a
LiFePO4 Lithium (Li) Iron (Fe) Phosphate (P4)
battery, as it's going to be lighter, keep it's voltage longer throughout a discharge cycle, and last longer.

How do you use the G90 with WSJT-X?

Simple. All you need is a DIN to 3.5mm cable and USB sound card. Find the cable on eBay. Please note that this item ships from China, not Canada. It's approximately $18 and a week and a half of waiting.

I've been recommended (and use) a Sabrent USB Sound Adapter. I got it for about $7 while on sale, normally it will be around $10.
