What's the deal with the globe?

It's cool, right? It's a globe that shows the contacts I've made so far. You can make yours at /globegenerator by uploading an ADIF file of your own.

Batteries not included, so use CTRL+SHIFT+U to see how I've setup the page.

What equipment do I use?

I have a Xiegu G90, and use a 7Ah lead acid battery for power. I'm trying to get a converted ATX powersupply going as a bench PSU, but haven't figured out the voltages quite yet.

I wouldn't recommend a lead acid battery. Use a LiFePO4 battery, as it's going to be lighter, keep it's voltage better, and last longer.

How do you use the G90 with WSJT-X?

Simple. All you need is a DIN to 3.5mm cable and USB sound card. Find the cable on eBay. Please note that this item ships from China, not Canada. It's approximately $18 and a week and a half of waiting.

I've been recommended (and use) a Sabrent USB Sound Adapter. I got it for about $7 while on sale, normally it will be around $10.
